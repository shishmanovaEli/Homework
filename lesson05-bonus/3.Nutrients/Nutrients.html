Nutrients

Nutrients are essential substances that organisms need to live, grow, and function properly.

Types of Nutrients

  • Macronutrients:
    • Carbohydrates
      • Rice
      • Potatoes
      • Quinoa
    • Proteins
      • Chicken
      • Beans
      • Fish
    • Fats (Lipids)
      • Avocado
      • Oil
      • Nuts
  • Micronutrients:
    • Vitamins
      • Water-soluble (e.g., vitamin C, B-complex vitamins)
      • Fat-soluble (e.g., vitamins A, D, E, K)
    • Minerals (e.g., calcium, potassium, iron, zinc, sodium)
  • Other:
    • Water
    • Dietary Fiber

Proper nutrition is crucial for maintaining health and supporting various physiological processes.
